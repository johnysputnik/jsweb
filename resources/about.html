about me

My name is John Cumming, I am an independent software engineering and development consultant based in the UK. I aim to deliver simple, effective, no nonsense solutions to real world business problems.

If you choose jsolutions, you can be guaranteed a pragmatic, efficient solution to your problem, with a focus on quality and reliability.

software development

I have 20 years of experience developing software using a large variety of languages and technologies and am able to adapt quickly to new opportunities presented by the rapidly developing nature of computing.

My primary skills are in C++ and Qt, for cross platform desktop UI driven applications and server software. I also have high levels of experience in Java, C# and .NET, HTML, CSS and JavaScript to name but a few.

I primarily work with simple database and XML based data sources and have a high level of experience with XSLT.

automated publishing

Over the past 10 years, I have been responsible for a large amount of research and development for Datazone, developing their automated publishing solution, Miramo.

As a result of this work, I have knowledge and experience in developing automated publishing solutions, with high levels of typographic fidelity in both Latin and Complex scripts, including Arabic, Thai and Indic scripts.
